About Hannah

"Designing confidence — one stitch, one stain at a time."

Based in Ilorin, Morenikeji Hannah brings together two crafts under one hand: natural, skin-safe henna art and custom fashion design. From bridal mehndi to ready-to-wear pieces, every design is made to fit the person wearing it — their skin, their style, their occasion. All ages, all occasions are welcome.
